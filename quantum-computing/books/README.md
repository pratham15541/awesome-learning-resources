# Quantum Computing Books

This folder contains the core books referenced by the quantum study plan.

## Recommended Order

1. [Quantum Computing for Everyone](quantum-computing-for-everyone.pdf)
2. [Programming Quantum Computers: Essential Algorithms and Code Samples](programming-quantum-computers-essential-algorithms-and-code-samples.pdf)
3. [Quantum Computing: A Gentle Introduction](Quantum%20Computing%20A%20Gentle%20Introduction%20%E2%80%94%20Eleanor%20Rieffel.pdf)
4. [Quantum Computing: An Applied Approach](Quantum%20Computing%20An%20Applied%20Approach%20by%20Jack%20D.%20Hidary.pdf)
5. [Quantum Computation and Quantum Information](quantum-computation-and-quantum-information-nielsen-chuang.pdf) (reference)

## Book Summaries

### Quantum Computing for Everyone

- File: [quantum-computing-for-everyone.pdf](quantum-computing-for-everyone.pdf)
- Intuitive explanation of qubits, superposition, and entanglement.
- Basic gate-level thinking without heavy prerequisites.
- Conceptual interpretation of measurement and probabilistic outcomes.
- Introductory view of quantum speedups and limitations.
- Beginner-friendly bridge from classical to quantum thinking.

### Programming Quantum Computers: Essential Algorithms and Code Samples

- File: [programming-quantum-computers-essential-algorithms-and-code-samples.pdf](programming-quantum-computers-essential-algorithms-and-code-samples.pdf)
- Practical circuit programming workflows for quantum SDKs.
- Construction of key subroutines such as phase kickback and QFT blocks.
- Implementation walkthroughs for canonical quantum algorithms.
- Techniques for simulation, debugging, and result interpretation.
- Coding-first approach to move from theory to executable circuits.

### Quantum Computing: A Gentle Introduction

- File: [Quantum Computing A Gentle Introduction — Eleanor Rieffel.pdf](Quantum%20Computing%20A%20Gentle%20Introduction%20%E2%80%94%20Eleanor%20Rieffel.pdf)
- Mathematical foundations needed for quantum algorithms.
- Formal treatment of states, operators, and measurements.
- Rigorous but accessible development of key algorithmic ideas.
- Complexity and computability context for quantum advantage.
- Smooth transition from introductory intuition to deeper theory.

### Quantum Computing: An Applied Approach

- File: [Quantum Computing An Applied Approach by Jack D. Hidary.pdf](Quantum%20Computing%20An%20Applied%20Approach%20by%20Jack%20D.%20Hidary.pdf)
- Application-driven perspective for optimization and machine learning.
- Hybrid quantum-classical workflow design patterns.
- Practical considerations for near-term hardware constraints.
- Industry-oriented examples and use-case framing.
- Roadmap from prototype experiments to real-world experimentation.

### Quantum Computation and Quantum Information

- File: [quantum-computation-and-quantum-information-nielsen-chuang.pdf](quantum-computation-and-quantum-information-nielsen-chuang.pdf)
- Comprehensive formal framework of quantum information science.
- Detailed theory behind quantum gates, circuits, and error models.
- Deep treatment of major algorithms and cryptographic protocols.
- Quantum error correction and fault-tolerance foundations.
- Long-term reference for advanced study and research depth.
